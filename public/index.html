KonoSuba Characters



Aqua (アクア, Akua)

Aqua is the goddess of water who judges humans to send to the RPG world up until Kazuma drags her along with him after she provokes him by mocking his death

Goddess of Water
Megumin (めぐみん)

Megumin is a 14 year-old archwizard who is part of the Crimson Demons race – modified humans who possess dark brown hair, crimson eyes, powerful magic affinity, and chunibyo characteristics.

Archwizard-Crimson Demon
Darkness (ダクネス, Dakunesu)

Darkness is an 18-year-old crusader who possesses powerful offense and defense but lacks the accuracy to deliver her attacks

Crusader masochist
Kazuma Sato (佐藤 和真, Satō Kazuma)

Kazuma is a 17-year-old game-obsessed shut-in, he is sent to a parallel reality and becomes a role-playing game character

Game-Obsessed Shut-In
